Rebelo\SaftPt\AuditFile\SourceDocuments\MovementOfGoods\MovementOfGoods

MovementOfGoods<br> The documents to be exported are any transport documents or delivery notes that serve as transport documents, as provided for under the “Regime de bens em Circulação” [Goods Circulation Regime], approved by the Decree No. 147/2003 of 11th July.

The documents listed under 4.1. –SalesInvoices also used as
transport documents (invoices for example) shall not be exported here.
