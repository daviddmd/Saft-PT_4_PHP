Get Tax Percentage<br> When it deals with a tax rate percentage it is required to fill in this field.

Percentage of the tax rate corresponding to the tax applicable to field 4.4.4.19.13. - DebitAmount or to field 4.1.4.19.14. - CreditAmount. In case of exemption or not subject to tax, fill in with “0” (zero).
