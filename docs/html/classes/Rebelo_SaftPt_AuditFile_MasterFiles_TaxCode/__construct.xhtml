TaxCode<br> In case field 2.5.1.1. – TaxType = IVA, the field must be filled in with:<br> “RED” – Reduced tax rate;<br> “INT” – Intermediate tax rate;<br> “NOR” – Normal tax rate;<br> “ISE” – Exempted;<br> “OUT” – Others, applicable to the special VAT regimes.<br> In case field 2.5.1.1. – TaxType = IS, it shall be filled in with:<br> The correspondent code of the Stamp Duty’s table;<br> “ISE” – Exempted.<br> In case it is not subject to tax it shall be filled in with “NS”.

In receipts issued without tax discriminated it shall be filled in with “NA”.
