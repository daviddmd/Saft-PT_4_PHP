Description of ProductType<br> The field shall be filled in with:<br> “P” - Products;<br> “S” - Services;<br> “O” - Others (e.g. charged freights, advance payments received or sale of assets);<br> “E” - Excise duties - (e.g. IABA, ISP, IT);<br> “I” - Taxes, tax rates and parafiscal charges except VAT and Stamp Duty which shall appear in table 2.5. – TaxTable and Excise Duties which shall be filled in with the "E" code.
