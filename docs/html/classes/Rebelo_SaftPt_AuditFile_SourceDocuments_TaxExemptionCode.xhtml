Rebelo\SaftPt\AuditFile\SourceDocuments\TaxExemptionCode

TaxExemptionCode<br> It shall be filled in with the code of the reason for exemption or non-settlement, which is included in the "Manual de Integração de Software – Comunicação das Faturas à AT" [Software Integration Manual - Communication of the Invoices to Tax and Customs Authority].

The filling is required when fields 4.1.4.19.15.4. - TaxPercentage
or 4.1.4.19.15.5. - TaxAmount are equal to zero.
This field shall also be filled in, for the cases not to subject to the
taxes mentioned in table 2.5. - TaxTable.
