Rebelo\SaftPt\AuditFile\SourceDocuments\SalesInvoices\InvoiceType

InvoiceType<br> The field shall be filled in with: “FT” - Invoice; “FS” - Simplified Invoice issued according to article 40 of the VAT code; “FR” – Invoice-receipt; “ND” - Debit note; “NC” - Credit note; “VD” - Sale for cash and invoice/sales ticket; (a) “TV” - Sale ticket; (a) “TD” - Devolution ticket; (a) “AA” - Assets sales; (a) “DA” - Assets returns. (a) For the Insurance sector when it must not be included in table 4.3. - WorkingDocuments, may also be filled in with: “RP” – Premium or premium receipt; “RE” - Return insurance or receipt of return insurance; “CS” - Imputation to co-insurance companies; “LD” - Imputation to a leader co-insurance company; “RA” - Accepted reinsurance. (a) For data up to 2012-12-31.
